About

Simulating Nicotine's Impact on Lung Cells

The project models how increasing levels of nicotine exposure influence cellular parameters such as inflammation (cytokine levels), oxidative stress, apoptosis, and metabolic activity. This simulation provides a detailed picture of the progressive changes in cellular health with rising nicotine concentrations.

Interactive Visualization

Users can adjust nicotine exposure levels and observe its effects on key cell health markers, including: Cytokine Levels: Indicators of inflammation. Viability: The percentage of living, functional cells. Oxidative Stress: Levels of reactive oxygen species (ROS) causing damage to cells. Metabolic Rate: Cellular energy production efficiency. Apoptosis Markers: Levels of programmed cell death.
